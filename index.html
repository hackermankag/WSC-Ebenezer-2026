Welcome
The Westminster Shorter Catechism was formulated in 1647 by the Westminster Assembly in London as a concise teaching tool for grounding believers in Reformed doctrine.

It summarizes key biblical truths in question-and-answer form, covering God, sin, Christ, salvation, and the Christian life.

This app presents one question per card. Try to recite the answer from memory, then tap Reveal answer to check yourself and move on.
